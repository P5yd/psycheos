BACKUP & DATA SAFETY
EXPORT YOUR DATA — NEVER LOSE A RECORD
💡 Recommended: Export a plain text backup after every session day. Saves to your Downloads folder as a readable .txt file you can open in any text editor, Word, or Notes.
PLAIN TEXT BACKUP — RECOMMENDED

Downloads a .txt file with all your students, sessions, risk notes, formulations, and assessments in plain readable text.

Last backup: —

ENCRYPTED BACKUP

Downloads a .psycheos file — encrypted with your password. Restore it into PSYCHE.OS on any computer.

HOW TO NEVER LOSE DATA
Daily habit
Click "Plain Text Backup" at the end of each working day. Takes 2 seconds. Store it in a folder on your Desktop.
Weekly
Download the encrypted .psycheos backup and copy it to your Google Drive, email it to yourself, or save to USB.
Important
This app stores data in your browser. Clearing browser history or cache will delete it. Your backups are your only safety net.
DANGER ZONE

Permanently erase all records. Export a backup first — this cannot be undone.
